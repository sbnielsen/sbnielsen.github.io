Bornö Workshop 2017

Southern Ocean

Topic

The Bornö Workshop 2017 centered around the Dynamics and Thermodynamics of the Southern Ocean.

Lectures by Trevor McDougall and Geoffrey Vallis (click names for download of notes).

Place and Time

Sunday, July 23 to Saturday, July 29 at Bornö Stationen, a small retreat in Sweden (see on map).
