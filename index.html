Welcome!
I am a part of Team Ocean in the Climate and Geophysics section and in close collaboration with the Center for Ice and Climate.
I have been employed at University of Copenhagen since February 2015.
My research focuses on ocean modelling, in particular on sub-grid mixing parameterizations.
Other research interests are past, current and future climate, high latitude oceanography, the ocean's role in the carbon cycle, inverse methods and ocean-ice interactions.
Apart from science I play guitar, enjoy movies and play board games.
I also enjoy reading books - recommendations are always appreciated!
