CARRA


Copernicus Arctic Regional ReAnalysis


The Copernicus Climate Change Arctic Regional Reanalysis is a 2.5 km horizontal resolution more than 30 year long reanalysis dataset covering two Areas: CARRA-EAST (Svalbard, Franz Josef Land, Novaya Zemlya, Barents Sea, and the Northern parts of the Norwegian Sea and Scandinavia) and CARRA-WEST (Greenland, the Labrador Sea, Davis Strait, Baffin Bay, Denmark Strait, Iceland, Jan Mayen, the Greenland Sea, and parts of Svalbard.)
CARRA Timely Updates is a near-real time update of the already existing CARRA dataset. Furthermore, CARRA2, covering a Pan-Arctic domain, is planned to go in production in Spring, 2024.

My contribution is participating in production of timely updates and working with output streams.
